Shipping & Returns

Shipping Policy

We are dedicated to getting your order to you as quickly and efficiently as possible. All orders are processed and dispatched from our studio within 1-3 business days.

Shipping Rates & Delivery

  • Standard Shipping: $10.00 flat rate. Estimated delivery within 5-7 business days after dispatch.
  • Free Shipping: All orders over $150 automatically qualify for free standard shipping.
  • Express Shipping: Currently unavailable. We are working to provide more options in the future.

Order Tracking

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number and a link to view the status of your delivery. Please allow up to 24 hours for the tracking information to become active.

Returns & Exchanges Policy

We want you to be completely in love with your purchase. If for any reason you are not satisfied, we are happy to accept returns within 30 days of the delivery date.

Conditions for Return

  • Items must be in their original, unworn, and unwashed condition.
  • All original tags and packaging must be attached and intact.
  • Items marked as "Final Sale" cannot be returned or exchanged.

How to Initiate a Return

To start a return, please visit our Contact Page and send us a message with your order number and the item(s) you wish to return. Our team will get back to you with detailed instructions.

Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your returned item(s), we will process your refund to the original payment method within 5-7 business days. Please note that original shipping fees are non-refundable. The cost of return shipping will be deducted from your refund.
